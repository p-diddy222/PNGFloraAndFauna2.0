Welcome to the Papua New Guinea Biodiversity database

This site was built for educational purposes to showcase the rich biodiversity of Papua New Guinea.
Papua New Guinea is a country consisting of the second largest island in the world and various smaller islands found in the south pacific.
It is home to an incredible array of unique species found nowhere else on Earth. On these site you can explore the diverse range of flora and fauna that make this region so unique. On the left navigation pane by clicking on the images you can navigate to different sections. Enjoy!
